Our Academic Program

Providing quality education from Pre-primary through Primary Seven

Curriculum Overview

Bright Angel Junior School follows the Uganda National Primary School Curriculum while incorporating innovative teaching methods to enhance learning. Our program is designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills.

Pre-primary (Nursery)

Ages 3-5 years

  • Early literacy and numeracy
  • Social and emotional development
  • Play-based learning
  • Creative arts

Primary 1-3

Lower Primary

  • Literacy (English )
  • Numeracy
  • Basic science concepts
  • Social studies
  • Religious education

Primary 4-7

Upper Primary

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Religious Education
  • Creative Arts and Physical Education

Our Teaching Approach

Learner-Centered

We focus on each child's individual needs and learning style, encouraging active participation.

Interactive Learning

Lessons incorporate discussions, group work, and hands-on activities.

Technology Integration

Our computer lab and smart classrooms enhance digital literacy.

Reading Emphasis

Daily reading programs build strong literacy skills.

Real-World Connections

We relate lessons to students' lives and community.

Continuous Assessment

Regular evaluations track progress and identify needs.

Special Programs

ICT Education

Our computer lab provides hands-on technology education starting from Primary Three, preparing students for the digital world.

Reading Program

The school library and reading clubs promote a love for reading with weekly reading sessions and book clubs.

Science Club

Hands-on experiments and projects develop scientific thinking and problem-solving skills.

School Garden

Students learn practical agriculture skills and environmental stewardship through our productive school garden.
