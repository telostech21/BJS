Our History

Bright Angel Junior School was founded in 2005 by a group of community leaders and educators in Bukoyo who recognized the need for a quality primary school in the area. What began as a small initiative with just 35 students has grown into one of the most respected primary schools in Iganga District.

Over the years, we've expanded our facilities, added new programs, and consistently achieved excellent academic results. Our commitment to holistic education has remained unchanged since our founding.
