Directions to Bright Angel Junior School

From Iganga Town:

  1. From the main road coming from kampala to Iganga
  2. Turn left at the Bright Angel signpost in CMS
  3. Drive for 50m from the maon road
  4. Turn right and you will see the school gate

Public transport: Take a taxi or boda boda to CMS and ask for directions to Bright Angel Junior School.
