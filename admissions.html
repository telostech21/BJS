Admissions Process

Join our community of learners and discover the Bright Angel difference

How to Apply

1

Inquiry

Contact us to learn more about the school and arrange a visit if desired.

2

Application

Complete the application form and submit with required documents.

3

Assessment

For Primary 2 and above, students complete a simple placement assessment.

4

Admission

Receive admission letter and payment details upon acceptance.

5

Orientation

Attend new student orientation before term begins.

Admission Requirements

Pre-primary (Nursery)

  • Child must be at least 3 years old
  • Birth certificate
  • 2 passport photos
  • Medical form

Primary 1

  • Child must be at least 6 years old
  • Birth certificate
  • 2 passport photos
  • Medical form
  • Previous school report (if applicable)

Primary 2 - Primary 7

  • Birth certificate
  • 2 passport photos
  • Medical form
  • Previous school report (if applicable)
  • Completion of placement assessment
